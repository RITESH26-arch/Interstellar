Who We Are

Interstellar is a space-focused platform built for explorers, students, and astronomy lovers. We combine real scientific data with modern technology to track and predict rare cosmic events like meteor showers, eclipses, and auroras.

🌠 Event Prediction

Accurate predictions for meteor showers, eclipses, and planetary events.

📸 Space Imagery

Curated images of galaxies, auroras, and deep space wonders.

🔔 Smart Alerts

Never miss a cosmic event with real-time alerts and updates.
